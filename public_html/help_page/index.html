Lets get Started!

Welcome to our website! We have two games to play, as well as a public leaderboard

Our first game is Wordle! In order to play, simply guess a 5 letter word. A black square means that the letter you guess is not in the final word, a yellow square means that letter is in the final word, but in the wrong place, and a green letter means you have the right letter in the right place. Guess the word to win!

Our second game is Connect 4! Play with you friends by entering their room code, and take turns dropping colored tiles into their slots. The first player to connect 4 tiles, either vertically, horizonally, or diagonally, wins!

Click the corresponding game card to get started!
